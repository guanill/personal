YourShare WebApp

YourShare is a project built as part of the New Technologist Program at Microsoft. Aimed at revitalizing the concept of sharing within communities. In a world dominated by corporate sharing platforms, YourShare focuses on bringing neighbors and friends together to share resources. Whether it's lending out tools or borrowing items for temporary use, YourShare promotes local connections, sustainability, and a sense of collective support. Join me in rediscovering the power of community sharing at YourShare.
